## Video formats

Limits: min `16:9`, max `9:16`.

### Portrait 1

- Res: `1080x1920`
- Aspect: `9:16`
- Safe area: top `14%`, bottom `35%`, sides `6%`
- Main fullscreen mobile format

### Portrait 2

- Res: `1080x1350`
- Aspect: `4:5`
- Smart Crop may reframe to this for feed

### Square

- Res: `1080x1080`
- Aspect: `1:1`
- Supported in feed + in-stream. Smart Crop target too

### Landscape

- Res: `1920x1080` or `1280x720`
- Aspect: `16:9`
- Still supported. Player can flip to landscape

## Encoding

- Container: `MP4` or `MOV`
- Video codec: `H.264`, max `4000px` wide, divisible by `16px`
- Audio codec: `AAC` stereo, `128 Kbps+`
- FPS: `<=30`
- Sample rate: `44.1 kHz`
- Bitrate: no universal target. Prioritize codec + res quality

## Limits

- Reels: no official length/orientation limit per 2025 update
- Legacy: max `240 min`, max `10 GB`
- File types: `MP4`, `MOV` best. `GIF` and others for ads too

## Style

- Mobile-first, vertical, fast pacing, high-energy edits
- Music from Facebook lib or original audio
- Voiceover + noise reduction common
- On-screen text + captions essential for sound-off feed
- Originality matters. Low-value edits get deprioritized

## Description

- Limit: `63,206` chars from third-party data. No published native `500`-char cap
- Strong hook text early
- Use relevant topics + hashtags
- Set visibility `Public` for max reach
- Mobile supports caption A/B test

## Thumbnails

- A/B test: up to `4` variants on mobile. Winner auto-shows
- Ads: Meta may auto-pick up to `3` frames, or allow custom upload in Ads Manager
- Cover / first frame is key creative hook for Explore

## Engagement

- Track: retention, avg watch time incl. replays, `1-minute` views, reactions, comments, shares
- Better retention + engagement = more organic distribution
- Test in Business Suite or Creator Studio, up to `4` variants
- Originality biggest multiplier. Reposts/unoriginal content suppressed
