## Video formats

Limits: aspect `1:2.4` to `2.4:1`.

### Portrait 1

- Res: `1080x1920`
- Aspect: `9:16`
- Safe area: keep edges clear. Unofficial overlay guess: top `108 px`, bottom `320 px`, left `60 px`, right `120 px`

### Portrait 2

- Res: `1080x1350`
- Aspect: `4:5`

### Square

- Res: `1080x1080`
- Aspect: `1:1`

### Landscape

- Res: `1920x1080` or `1200x675`
- Aspect: `16:9`

## Encoding

- Container: `MP4` safest. Needed if later sponsored
- Video codec: `H.264` or `VP8`
- Audio codec: `AAC` or `MPEG4` under `64 KHz`
- FPS: `10-60`. Use `30`
- Bitrate: `192 Kbps-30 Mbps`

## Limits

- File: `75 KB` min, `5 GB` max
- Formats: `MP4`, `MOV`, `AVI`, `WEBM`, `MKV`, `WMV`, `VC1`, `MPEG/MPEG2/MPEG1VIDEO`, `DVVIDEO`, `QTRLE`, `TSCC2`
- ProRes: not recommended
- Length: `3s` min desktop / `2s` min mobile, `15 min` max
- Best organic length: under `60s`
- Best sponsored length: `15-30s`

## Style

- Mobile-first, face-led: direct camera, demos, event footage
- Tight pacing. Hook in first `3-6s`
- Authentic beats overproduced corporate
- Minimal intro bumpers
- Always include captions. Feed autoplay muted

## Description

- Limit: `3000` chars
- Tease video early
- Use `1-5` relevant CamelCase hashtags, natural or end
- External links hurt reach. Reported penalty about `26.5%-38%`

## Thumbnails

- Custom upload supported on desktop
- Use `JPG` or `PNG`, max `2 MB`
- Thumb aspect + res must match video
- A/B test: not official for organic. Mostly paid ads only

## Engagement

- Track: avg watch time, watch time per view, reactions/comments/reposts per view, saves per impression
- First `6s` critical for retention
- Use video as part of mixed-format strategy: docs, multi-image, text. Not only format
