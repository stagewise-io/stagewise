## Video formats

Limits: min `1.91:1`, max `9:16`.

### Portrait 1

- Res: `1080x1920`
- Aspect: `9:16`
- Safe area: top `250px`, bottom `340px`. Keep text centered
- Best for Reels, Stories, fullscreen

### Portrait 2

- Res: `1080x1350`
- Aspect: `4:5`
- Best for max feed space without fullscreen

### Square

- Res: `1080x1080`
- Aspect: `1:1`
- Best for legacy grid consistency

### Landscape

- Res: `1080x565` approx
- Aspect: `1.91:1`
- Best for cinematic/horizontal-only. Usually weaker on Reels

## Encoding

- Container: `MP4` or `MOV`
- FPS: `23-60`. Use `30`
- Video bitrate: `<=25 Mbps`
- Audio bitrate: `<=128 kbps`, `AAC` preferred

## Limits

- File: `300 MB` API/scheduler safe, up to `4 GB` manual upload
- Formats: `MP4`, `MOV`, `GIF` stories only
- Reels: `3s-3min` safe default, some flows up to `15 min`
- Stories: `1-60s` per clip
- Carousel: `3s-60min`
- Live: up to `4h`

## Style

- Vertical, high-res, short-form
- Fast cuts, in-app effects, trim, reorder
- High-quality original audio, voiceover, or trending/library music
- Avoid blurry footage, borders, recycled watermarked content
- Use captions/subtitles for mute-viewing + SEO

## Description

- Limit: `2200` chars
- First `125` chars before more-link must carry payoff or CTA
- Hashtags: `3-5` highly relevant
- Links not clickable in captions. Plain text only

## Thumbnails

- Pick frame from video or upload custom `1080x1920` image
- Center important elements for `3:4` / `1:1` grid crops
- No direct thumb A/B test note here. Trial Reels used for audience testing before wider distribution

## Engagement

- Primary metric: views
- Top signal: sends per reach, shares
- Retention after first `3s` matters. Check retention charts
- Native/original content prioritized. Aggregator/recycled content deprioritized
- First frame must arrest. It becomes default feed thumb
