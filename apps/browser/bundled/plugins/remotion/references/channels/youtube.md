## Video formats

Limits: desktop standard `16:9`. Player adapts to upload ratio. No baked-in black bars.

### Landscape

- Res: `1920x1080`, `3840x2160`, `7680x4320`
- Aspect: `16:9`
- Lower res: `1440p`, `720p`, `480p`, `360p`, `240p`

### Square / Vertical

- Res: `1080x1080`, `1080x1920`
- Aspect: `1:1` or `9:16`
- Upload after `2024-10-15`: square/vertical under `3 min` auto-classed as Shorts. For long-form, use `16:9`

## Encoding

- Container/video/audio: `MP4`, `H.264`, `AAC-LC` or `Opus`
- Audio: `48 kHz`, stereo `384 kbps` or `5.1 512 kbps`
- Video: progressive, High Profile, `2` B-frames, closed GOP, `4:2:0`
- FPS: match source: `24,25,30,48,50,60`. Deinterlace first
- 1080p bitrate: `8 Mbps` at `24-30 fps`, `12 Mbps` at `48-60 fps`
- 4K bitrate: `35-45 Mbps` at `24-30 fps`, `53-68 Mbps` at `48-60 fps`

## Limits

- Length: `15 min` unverified, `12 h` verified
- File: `256 GB` max
- Formats: `.MOV .MP4 .AVI .WMV .MPEGPS .FLV 3GPP WebM ProRes HEVC`
- Audio-only unsupported. Convert `MP3/WAV` to video

## Style

- Thumbnail + title must match content
- Use pro captions or edited auto-captions
- Keep sustainable posting cadence

## Description

- Limit: `5000` chars
- First lines matter most. Put `1-2` keywords in title + description
- Use for chapters, playlist links, collaborator credits, channel links
- Tags low priority. Mostly for misspellings
- Hashtags: max `60`. Over limit ignores all. No spaces

## Thumbnails

- Specs: `16:9`, min `640px` wide, `<2MB` mobile or `<50MB` desktop, `JPG GIF PNG`
- A/B: `Test & Compare`, up to `3` variants, desktop only, advanced features needed
- Vertical note: `16:9` thumbs may auto-crop to `4:5` on mobile

## Engagement

- Search signals: relevance, engagement, quality
- Discovery tracks watch history, searches, likes/dislikes, not-interested markers
- Watch CTR on Home vs Subscriptions in first `24h`
- No fake engagement or inflated metrics
