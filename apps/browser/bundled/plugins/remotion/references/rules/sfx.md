To include sound effect, use `<Audio>` tag.

```tsx
import{Audio}from "@remotion/sfx";
<Audio src={"https://remotion.media/whoosh.wav"}/>;
```

Available sound effects in `https://remotion.media/{file}.wav`: whoosh,whip,page-turn,switch,mouse-click,shutter-modern,shutter-old,ding,bruh,vine-boom,windows-xp-error

For more, search internet. Good source: <https://github.com/kapishdima/soundcn/tree/main/assets>.
