## Video formats

Limits: must be square `1:1` or vertical `9:16`.

### Portrait

- Res: `1080x1920`
- Aspect: `9:16`
- Safe area house rule: keep key text/visuals away from top `30%` and bottom `20%`

### Square

- Res: `1080x1080`
- Aspect: `1:1`

## Encoding

- Specs: `H.264 MP4`, `AAC-LC 48 kHz`, native fps
- Bitrate: `8-12 Mbps` for `1080p`
- Max res: `1080p`

## Limits

- Length: up to `3 min`
- Title: `100` chars max
- Copyright: Shorts `>1 min` with Content ID claims blocked globally, no monetization
- Audio Library: standard songs allowed for `30`, `60`, or `90s`, varies by track
- Links: clickable Related Video link can be added in Studio

## Style

- Hook in first `1s` or user swipes
- Fast cuts, clear story, on-screen text/captions
- Voiceover helps. Trending Audio Library sounds help too
- Authentic + original beats generic trend copy

## Description

- Limit: `5000` chars
- Focus on search keywords
- Outbound links allowed, but must follow safety rules
- Hashtags relevant. Top vs bottom placement no proven effect

## Thumbnails

- No external custom image upload
- Pick frame during upload in YouTube app
- Cannot change after publish
- No A/B testing for Shorts

## Engagement

- Watch shown-in-feed vs chose-to-view, aka swipe-away
- Since `2025-03-31`, view counts when playback starts
- Use Engaged Views for better benchmarking
- Reply to comments with Short for engagement
- Ranking uses personalization, AVD, percent viewed
